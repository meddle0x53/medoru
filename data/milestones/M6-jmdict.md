# Milestone 6: JMdict Integration (Words)

**Status:** ⏳ PLANNED  
**Date:** TBD  
**Priority:** Medium

## Overview

Integrate JMdict for comprehensive Japanese-English dictionary data including vocabulary words and example sentences.

## Goals

1. Download JMdict
2. Parse word entries
3. Cross-reference with kanji
4. Export vocabulary lists by JLPT level

## Data to Include

- Word entries
- Readings (kana)
- Meanings/definitions
- Part of speech
- JLPT level (if available)
- Example sentences
